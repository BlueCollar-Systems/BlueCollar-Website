Privacy Policy

Steel Logic — Structural Steel Shapes Reference App

Effective Date: April 6, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 6, 2026

BlueCollar Systems ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Steel Logic mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use the App.

We are committed to keeping things straightforward. Steel Logic is a reference and calculator tool—it does not require an account, login, or any personal data entry to function.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Steel Logic does not directly collect personal information from you. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login is required.
  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers are requested.
  • No user-entered data is transmitted from your device to our servers.

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • User preferences (units, display settings, theme).
  • Favorite shapes you have bookmarked.
  • Recently viewed shapes for quick access.

This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us, and is deleted when you uninstall the App or clear its data.

3. Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may automatically collect certain device and usage data:

a. Firebase Analytics (Google)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand general usage patterns such as which features are used most and how the App performs. Firebase Analytics may collect device identifiers, usage statistics, and general location data (country/region level). No personally identifiable information is collected through Firebase Analytics.

Firebase Privacy Information

b. Firebase Crashlytics (Google)

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostics so we can identify and fix bugs. Crashlytics collects device state information, crash stack traces, and a Crashlytics installation UUID. It does not collect personal information.

Firebase Privacy Information

c. Google AdMob

The App displays banner advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and general location data to serve and measure advertisements. Ad personalization depends on your device settings and Google account preferences. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's settings.

Google Privacy Policy

4. How We Use Collected Information

The limited data collected through third-party services is used to:

  • Monitor and improve App stability and performance.
  • Understand which features are most valuable to users.
  • Display advertisements to support continued free development.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties. The only data shared is what Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google AdMob collect automatically as described above, subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Offline Functionality

Steel Logic's core features—shape data, properties, and calculators—work fully offline. An internet connection is only required for displaying advertisements and transmitting analytics/crash data.

7. Children's Privacy

Steel Logic is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

8. Your Choices

  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your Android device settings under Google > Ads.
  • Analytics: You can limit analytics data collection by adjusting your device privacy settings.
  • Local Data: You can clear all locally stored preferences and favorites by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
